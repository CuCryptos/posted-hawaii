This is it. The first one.

Drop 001 is five pieces built from everything we stand on — presence, craft, and the crew that holds it all together. Every piece is named after a spot on O'ahu because that's where this started.

## The Lineup

**The Sandy's Tee** — Named after Sandy Beach. Raw, powerful, no filter. Heavyweight 6oz cotton, oversized fit, ribbed collar that won't stretch out. Available in Black and Teal.

**The Town Tee** — Rep town. Clean coral hit on premium cotton. The one that starts conversations.

**The Ala Moana Tee** — Clean. Classic. Minimal POSTED mark on the chest, full lockup on the back. The everyday tee that goes with everything.

**The OG Cap** — The staple snapback. Structured crown, flat brim, embroidered POSTED lockup. One size fits most.

**The Crew Cap** — Unstructured dad cap. Washed cotton, curved brim, embroidered P mark. Low-key posted.

## Details

Every tee is 6oz heavyweight combed ringspun cotton. Oversized relaxed fit. Screen-printed graphics that hold up wash after wash. We tested these for months before we put our name on them.

The caps are embroidered, not printed. Because details matter.
