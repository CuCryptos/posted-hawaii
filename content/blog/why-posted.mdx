We didn't sit down and write a business plan. There was no pitch deck. No investor meetings. No "brand strategy session."

We just kept showing up to the same spots, wearing the same brands that never quite felt like us.

## The Problem

Every streetwear brand repping Hawai'i was either too tourist or too exclusive. Too much aloha-spirit-sunset-paradise energy, or too much gatekeeping. Nothing in between.

We wanted something that looked like a Saturday morning surf check at Sandy's. Like a late-night food run to Chinatown. Like pulling up to the beach park and your whole crew is already there.

## The Name

POSTED means you're here. You're present. You're not somewhere else, you're not on your phone — you're posted up with your people.

That's all we wanted the brand to feel like. You know where to find us.

## Bold With Discipline

That's the rule. We don't do loud for the sake of loud. We don't do minimal for the sake of minimal. Every piece has to pass one test:

> Would your crew rock this?

If the answer isn't an immediate yes, we go back to the drawing board.

## What's Next

Drop 001 launches April 1. Five pieces. The tees, the caps, the foundation. Everything after this builds on what we start here.

Mahalo for being part of it.
